## Brand Campaign — Scenario Prompt Addendum

When the scenario is **brand_campaign**, follow these additional guidelines:

### Tone & Style
- Use a formal, polished brand voice — authoritative yet approachable, never casual
- Avoid first-person storytelling; use third-person or inclusive "we" voice
- Maintain multi-layer review compliance awareness (legal, regulatory, brand)
- Brand guidelines must be woven naturally into the narrative, not recited as bullet points
- Every claim must be defensible with evidence or attribution

### Content Focus
- Emphasise brand heritage, product quality, and design philosophy
- Show, don't tell — let premium visuals do the talking
- Focus on the lifestyle and identity the brand represents
- Include aspirational lifestyle moments that position the product as essential
- Reference clinical data, certifications, and awards when applicable, but integrate them seamlessly

### Platform-Specific Requirements

#### Shopify PDP Video (30-60s)
- Showcase product hero shots with clean, minimalist aesthetics
- Integrate key USPs through text overlays and visual callouts (not voiceover lists)
- Include lifestyle-in-context b-roll (e.g. pumping discreetly while working, packing for travel)
- Closed captioning for mute-by-default playback
- CTA: "Shop Now" with prominent button overlay
- Format: 1:1 or 4:5 for inline product page embed
- Audio: Professional voiceover with ambient underscore

#### Amazon A+ / EBC Video (30-90s)
- Trust-focused: lead with certifications (FDA-cleared, CE marked), clinical study results, and real-mom testimonials
- Side-by-side comparison segments showing functional advantages over competitors
- Problem-solution narrative arc: identify a pain point, introduce X1 as the answer
- Data-driven substantiation for every performance claim
- CTA: "See why 50,000+ moms trust X1 — Learn More"
- Format: 16:9 for A+ Enhanced Brand Content module
- Audio: Polished professional voiceover; no background music that competes with narration

#### YouTube Shorts (15-60s)
- Hook in the first 2 seconds with a visually striking brand moment
- Premium production feel — cinematic lighting, intentional composition
- Storytelling arc with clear beginning, middle, and resolution
- Less text-heavy than TikTok; let the imagery carry the narrative
- CTA: "Subscribe for more" + "Shop the collection" (end screen elements)
- Format: 9:16 vertical or 1:1 square
- Audio: Full mix — professional VO + carefully selected music bed + SFX

#### Instagram Reel / Feed (15-60s)
- Highly curated visual aesthetic consistent with brand feed grid
- Carousel-style storytelling if using feed post format
- Reels: quick cuts, trend-aware editing, still polished but approachable
- Use brand fonts and colour palette in all text overlays
- CTA: "Link in bio" + "Save for later" for reach
- Format: 9:16 vertical (Reels), 1:1 or 4:5 (feed)
- Audio: Trending audio mixed with professional VO or brand audio signature

#### Brand Site Hero Video (60-90s)
- Cinematic opening shot establishing brand world / lifestyle
- Full brand story narrative: mission → problem → solution → transformation
- Minimal text overlay — let the footage speak
- Hero moment: product reveal or signature usage shot
- Emotional arc designed to build brand affinity, not just drive a transaction
- CTA: "Explore X1" with subtle animated button or "Learn More" overlay
- Format: 16:9 widescreen, 4K resolution minimum
- Audio: Custom score or licensed track + professional VO; full sound design
- Note: This is the highest-production-value asset in the campaign
