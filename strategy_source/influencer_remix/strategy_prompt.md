## Influencer Remix — Scenario Prompt Addendum

When the scenario is **influencer_remix**, follow these additional guidelines:

### Tone & Style
- Use first-person perspective ("I switched to this pump and here's what happened...")
- Authentic, personal storytelling — not corporate marketing
- Include personal anecdotes and relatable moments
- Voice should match the creator's personal brand (empowering, relatable, honest)

### Content Focus
- Each brief should be a specific "angle" a creator would use naturally:
  - "As a working mom, here's how I pump during Zoom calls without anyone knowing"
  - "I tried 5 wearable pumps — here's why I'm keeping this one"
  - "Real talk: pumping at work is awkward, but this changed everything"
- Highlight product USPs naturally within the story, not as feature lists

### Platform-Specific Requirements

#### Shopify PDP Video (30-60s)
- Product-focused: show the pump in use, cleaning, fitting in a bag
- Feature USPs with text overlays (hands-free, 40dB, hospital-grade)
- Direct CTA: "Shop the X1 — link below"
- Format: 1:1 or 4:5 for product page embed

#### Amazon A+ / EBC Video (30-90s)
- Trust-focused: certifications (FDA, CE), clinical data, real mom reviews
- Comparison: side-by-side with traditional pumps
- CTA: "See why 50,000+ moms chose X1"
- Format: 16:9 for A+ module

#### TikTok Video (15-45s)
- Hook-first: most important 1.5 seconds
- Authentic, raw feel — not overly produced
- Personal storytelling with product as the solution
- Bio link CTA: "Link in bio for details"
- Hashtags: #wearablepump #pumpingmom #workingmom
- Format: 9:16 vertical

#### Reddit Post (text + optional video)
- Community-first tone: "I was skeptical about wearable pumps until I tried..."
- Genuine recommendation, not an ad
- Include specific pros/cons
- Product link naturally embedded in post
- Follow subreddit rules (no direct advertising)
