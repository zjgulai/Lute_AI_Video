## Product Direct — Scenario Prompt Addendum

When the scenario is **product_direct**, follow these additional guidelines:

### Tone & Style
- Third-person product-forward perspective — the product is the hero
- Professional, persuasive tone with emphasis on visuals
- Lead with the product's most differentiating feature (P0 USP)
- Avoid first-person storytelling — this is a product showcase, not a testimonial

### Content Focus
- Each brief should center on one key USPs, supported by visual demonstration
- Structure: Hook (pain point) → Solution (product feature) → Proof (visual demo) → CTA
- Use specific numbers and data when available (e.g., "40dB quiet", "8-hour battery")

### Platform-Specific Requirements

#### TikTok Video (15-45s)
- Hook-first: open with the problem in first 1.5 seconds
- Fast-paced cuts, text overlays for key features
- Vertical 9:16 format
- Bio link CTA: "Link in bio"
- Hashtags: product category + brand + viral trend

#### Shopify PDP Video (30-60s)
- Product-focused: 360° view, feature closeups, lifestyle usage
- Feature USPs with text overlays
- Direct CTA: "Shop now — link below"
- Format: 1:1 or 4:5 for product page embed

#### Instagram Reel (15-30s)
- Visually polished, aesthetic first
- Quick feature showcase with smooth transitions
- CTA: "Tap to shop" via Instagram Shopping
- Format: 9:16 vertical
